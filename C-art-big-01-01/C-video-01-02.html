Midnight Run in the City

Voice-over Narrative：

The city where the film shot is called Hangzhou. Hangzhou is my hometown and used to be an imperial city a thousand years ago.

The city has been changed big during the rapid expansion of the city in the past decades. It has become modern and yet strange and barren. There are something missing but I can’t tell what they are.

In the movie, as I running through the city at midnight, I feel like wondering in the labyrinths not knowing way out. Everything appear mysterious.....
